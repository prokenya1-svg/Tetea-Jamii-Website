Our Programs

Digital Justice Hub

Bridging the digital divide in legal access, our hubs provide computers, internet, and expert guidance to navigate Kenya's e-court system. We help clients file cases digitally, attend virtual hearings, and connect with pro bono legal representation.

Clients Served: 500+ monthly  |  Target: 3 Hubs by 2025

ESG & Business Justice

Holding corporations accountable while helping communities understand their rights. We monitor environmental compliance, provide legal support for affected communities, and help businesses develop ethical frameworks that respect human rights.

Community Monitors: 200+  |  Corporate Partners: 5+

Women's Justice Leadership

Empowering women and girls from survivors to changemakers. We provide rapid legal response for GBV cases, economic justice support for property rights, and leadership training to create the next generation of female legal advocates.

Women Served: 300+ annually  |  Success Rate: 85%

Join Our Justice Movement

Your skills can change lives. Whether you're a legal professional, tech expert, or community organizer, we have a place for you. Join our dedicated team of volunteers and help us build a more just society, one case at a time.
